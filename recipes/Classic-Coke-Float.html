Classic Coke Float

Ingredients

  • 1 Scoop Vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 (12-ounce) can Coca-Cola

Instructions

  1. Place scoop of vanilla ice cream in the bottom of a tall glass.
  2. Pour 1/2 can of Coca-Cola over the top of the ice cream.
  3. Insert a straw and enjoy!
